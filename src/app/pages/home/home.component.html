Leaks are among the most common plumbing issues that homeowners experience. They are also one of the most damaging. As water continues to flow in areas of your home that it’s not supposed to, the damage can be extensive. Leaks are not only hard to detect, but they are also challenging to find. Unless the leak comes from one of the few pipes in your home that you can see, you’ll likely not know where it originates.
